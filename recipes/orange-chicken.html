Rosemary Orange Chicken

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Butter
  • Honey
  • Orange juice
  • White vinegar
  • Soy sauce
  • Cornstarch
  • Chicken thighs
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Rosemary

Steps

  1. Cut chicken thighs into 1 inch cubes.
  2. Marinate chicken 24 hours in brine of water, salt, sugar, garlic, ginger, and rosemary.
  3. Cook chicken as desired.
  4. In sauce pan, heat a small amount of butter together with garlic and ginger.
  5. After a minute, add orange juice, soy sauce, honey, and vinegar to pan and mix well.
  6. Make a slurry with cornstarch and water. Add to sauce to thicken.
  7. Combine sauce and cooked chicken.
