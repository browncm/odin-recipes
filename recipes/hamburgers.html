Simple Hamburgers

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Ground beef
  • Sliced cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Form ground beef into several small balls.
  2. Preheat a pan.
  3. Press balls of ground beef into pan. Do not overcrowd.
  4. Let hamburger cook for two minutes before flipping.
  5. Cook for another two minutes and remove.
  6. Wash lettuce and tomato, and slice tomato.
  7. Assemble hamburger with buns, meat, lettuce, tomato, and condiments.
